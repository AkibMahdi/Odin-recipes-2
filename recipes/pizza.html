pizza

Description

The following dish known as "Pizza" consists of a few simple ingredients: Tomato sauce, Mozzarella cheese, and Dough. Although there are different variations of "pizza", the 3 ingredients listed makes up the popular variation of the dish.

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • dough
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Spread out dough and pour the sauce over
  3. once sauce covers dough surface, add cheese
  4. place the dish inside oven to bake
  5. Boom! Done!
